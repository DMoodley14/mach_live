What is MACH?

MACH (Multilingual AI Chatbot delivering information on HIV) is a chatbot trained to provide reliable, evidence-based information about HIV.

Uses a large language model (LLM) fine-tuned specifically for HIV-related topics, ensuring responses are accurate, relevant, and sensitive to users' needs.

In addition, MACH supports multiple languages through machine translation, making it accessible to a diverse range of users worldwide.

We deploy robust evaluation frameworks and protocols designed to verify that MACH functions as intended while remaining accurate, safe, fair, equitable, actionable, and evidence-based.

MACH is designed to support, not replace, professional medical advice. It offers accessible health education that complements the care provided by healthcare professionals.

Why We Created MACH

Access to accurate HIV information should not depend on where you live, the language you speak, or whether you feel comfortable asking for help.

Yet, stigma, misinformation, and language barriers continue to make it difficult for many people to find the answers they need.

MACH was created to change that ‚Äî combining the expertise of clinicians, people with lived experience, and AI developers to create a tool that offers trusted information with empathy and respect. Our goal is simple: to empower people to make informed, confident decisions about their health.
