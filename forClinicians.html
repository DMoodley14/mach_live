MACH: Supporting HIV Education and Patient Care

A multilingual, evidence-informed chatbot designed to provide accessible, accurate HIV information for patients and the public — supporting clinicians in improving health literacy and engagement.

Under the MACH hood

MACH uses a proprietary large language model (LLM) trained on trusted HIV-related data and integrated with machine translation to provide accurate answers about HIV prevention, treatment, and living well with HIV. It supports clinical care by guiding patients to reliable information and enhancing health literacy.

Multilingual HIV Education

Content available in multiple languages and literacy levels to serve diverse patient populations.

24/7 Patient Access

Reliable answers between appointments, supporting continuous patient engagement.

Evidence-Based Content

Aligned with NHS, WHO, UNAIDS and BHIVA guidance for clinical accuracy.

Multi-Platform Access

Accessible on web and mobile, designed for low-bandwidth environments.

Reduces Stigma & Misinformation

Non-judgmental responses tailored to promote safer behaviors and accurate understanding.

Governance & Security

Clinical Governance & Data Protection

MACH is developed in accordance with GDPR and NHS Digital information governance standards. Conversations are encrypted and anonymous; identifiable patient data is neither stored nor used to train models. Clinical content is validated and regularly reviewed by HIV specialists.

Clinical Assurance Processes

Clinical governance processes include specialist review, change control for content, and documented escalation pathways for clinical queries flagged by the chat system.

• Specialist reviewer panels for content validation
• Formal change-control for content updates
• Escalation pathways for flagged clinical queries
