Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MACH.

Is MACH free to use?
Yes! MACH is completely free to use for individuals. We believe in accessible HIV health support for everyone.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We do not store your conversations. Everything is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after your session ends.
Can I use MACH on my smartphone?
Yes, MACH is fully responsive and works beautifully on all mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
Does MACH replace my healthcare provider?
No. MACH is an AI-powered support tool designed to provide information about HIV. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
